# Foogie_2.0

An ios app designed to keep track on user's inventory in their fridge. 
User can upload photos of grocery they bought to record down their expiration date, and also keep track on the ingredient in there. 

User can also use the app to upload photos of the dishes they make, to create their own "menu", which collects their ideas of cooking customized dishes. 

Expectation: make a "moment" section where user can post their new ideas of dishes or shared the dishes they have made. Other users can choose to like their post.
